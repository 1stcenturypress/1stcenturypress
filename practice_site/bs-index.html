About BBS

What Makes BBS Different?

We are a conservative and faith-based development group located in the good ol' U.S.A.

We may be a new company, but we really began designing websites in the 1990s, during the very infancy of the "world wide web". Yes, we remember AOL, Netscape, Internet Explorer, RealPlayer and Adobe Flash... as cutting-edge technologies. We used 3.5 and 5.25 disks, dialed-up our accounts, and dreamed of DSL.

Some of our earliest designs pre-dated CSS, XML, and even JavaScript. We even survived Y2K and mourned the death of Napster.

We stepped back for a few years, raised our families, and let the next generation take control for awhile. But now we're back in the development game.

A whole world of things have changed online. The Internet has seen 5 major overhauls of its standard design guidelines and countless micro-changes virtually every day over the last 25 years. There really isn't any aspect of the world we know that doesn't require some site, app, or coding.

We have become skilled in some of the latest and greatest coding languages for web and app development, both front- and back-end.
