GRADIENT BACKGROUNDS

Two types of Gradients: Linear and Radial

Linear goes from side to side and Radial emerges from the center. Linear is the predominant style ad will be our focus.

The format is simply BACKGROUND-IMAGE: LINEAR-GRADIENT(DIRECTION, COLOR, COLOR). And you can see an example here with a direction TO RIGHT of BLACK to YELLOW.

We can also assign it in degrees (DEG) like this one at 45DEG.

There are several Gradient Generator sites. CSSGradient.io and Colorzilla.com produce ones like these:

Of course, you can combine the effects of gradients and background into layers. See this sample page .
