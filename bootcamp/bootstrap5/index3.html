Containers

Containers are the essential Bootstrap building blocks. Consider them to be the modern replacement of tables. but far more dynamic and responsive. The basic concept is to treat each container as if it consists of 12 blocks.

Here's a standard, evenly balanced container of 3 "columns", each consuming 4 of the 12 blocks.

Here we've defined the first "column" to consume 7 of the 12 blocks. The second 3 blocks and third 2 blocks. For responsive design, we have indicated col-md-x for each. When the site drops below medium size, the columns convert to separate full rows.
