Display

Syntax for display is simple:

d-{value}

where the values are none, inline, inline-block, block, grid, table, table-cell, table-row, flex, and inline-flex.

So d-line produces:

d-inline p-2 bg-primary text-white
d-inline p-2 bg-dark text-white

and d-block produces:

d-block p-2 bg-primary text-white d-block p-2 bg-dark text-white

For responsive design, there are more variants:

  • d-none (displays on none)
  • d-sm-inline (displays on small up)
  • d-md-inline (displays on medium up)
  • d-lg-inline (displays on large up)
  • d-xl-inline (displays on extra large up)
  • d-xxl-inline (displays on xxl only)
  • d-inline (displays on all sizes)
  • d-none d-sm-inline d-none (displays on only small)
  • d-md-inline d-xxl-none (displays on medium up to but excluding xxl)
  • ...and so on
